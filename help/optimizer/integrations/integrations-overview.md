# Integrations

[!DNL Adobe Commerce Optimizer] works with several Adobe and partner integrations so you can sync commerce data, manage assets, improve storefront experiences, and connect external systems. Use the sections below to understand how each integration fits with [!DNL Adobe Commerce Optimizer] and follow the links for setup, configuration, and day-to-day use.

## Adobe Commerce Optimizer Connector

The Adobe Commerce Optimizer Connector is the bridge that synchronizes catalog and pricing data between Adobe Commerce (cloud or on-premises) and [!DNL Adobe Commerce Optimizer]. When the connector is enabled, Commerce remains the system of record for product data while [!DNL Adobe Commerce Optimizer] powers AI search, recommendations, merchandising rules, analytics, and headless storefront experiences.

* [Adobe Commerce Optimizer Connector overview](../../aco-connector/overview.md)
* [Get started with the connector](../../aco-connector/get-started.md)

## AEM Assets for Adobe Commerce Optimizer

The AEM Assets integration connects Adobe Experience Manager Assets with [!DNL Adobe Commerce Optimizer] so product imagery and marketing assets stay centralized, approved, and in sync with your catalog. Assets are matched to products (for example by SKU) and updates flow through Adobe’s integration services so storefronts reflect the latest media without manual re-uploads.

* [AEM Assets Integration for Commerce overview](../../aem-assets-integration/overview.md)
* [Configure AEM Assets for Commerce Optimizer](../../aem-assets-integration/get-started/configure-aco.md)

## Adobe Experience Manager Sites Optimizer

Adobe Experience Manager Sites Optimizer analyzes commerce and content signals to surface AI-driven **Opportunities**—recommendations that help you improve discovery, engagement, and conversions. This capability is tied to your Adobe licensing: when your Adobe account is provisioned with the **Ultima** Adobe Sites Optimizer license, the integration is available for your tenant; enablement is coordinated with Adobe (for example through your customer success manager) as described in the Opportunities documentation.

* [Opportunities](../manage-results/opportunities.md)

## Commerce Salesforce Connector

The Commerce Salesforce Connector (built on Adobe App Builder) syncs catalog and price data from Salesforce Commerce Cloud B2C into [!DNL Adobe Commerce Optimizer] so you can use Adobe storefront and merchandising capabilities without replatforming your Salesforce commerce backend. You can schedule syncs, run on-demand updates, and extend the integration using Salesforce Commerce APIs.

* [Salesforce Commerce Connector](../developer/salesforce-connector.md)
